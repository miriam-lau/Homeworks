Ingredients

  • 1 hamburger roll, buttered and toasted
  • 4 oz fresh ground beef chuck
  • 1 slice American cheese
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Choice of condiments

Smash Burger

The Ultimate Smash Burger Recipe, not as Good as Lopez-Alt's...

Description

Bring the classic ShakeShack burger to your kitchen.

Directions

  1. Preheat the baking steel.
  2. Split beef into 2, 2-oz balls and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Place each beef ball onto the baking steel, and smash down with a stiff metal spatula.
  4. Cook for about 45 seconds, then flip patties over.
  5. Add a slice of cheese to each patty and stack one on top of the other.
  6. Place on top of the burger bun. Add condiments.
  7. Eat!

Original recipe by J.Kenji Lopez-Alt
